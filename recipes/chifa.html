Chifa Peruano

A product of Chinese immigration in Peru during the mid-1800s, the Chifa our Chaufa is today a culinary symbol of the country

Making a Chifa is very simple, usually needing no more than 20 minutes if one has already pree-coked rice at hand

Ingredients

  • White Rice
  • Green Onion
  • Onion
  • Soy Sauce
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Chicken Breast
  • Eggs
  • Ginger
  • Red Pepper
  • Pepper
  • Bacon

Steps

  1. Cook the rice and let it cool in a bowl
  2. Boil chicken breast to the point right before it stops being raw
  3. Mix egg yolks and fry one big omelette, then chop into small portions
  4. Cut the semi-boiled chicken into cubes
  5. In a wok, add some oil and start frying some ginger as well as some onion cubes
  6. After two minutes, add the chicken cubes
  7. After 5-7 minutes, add the rice, the sliced red pepper, the bacon and the chopped green onion
  8. After 3-4 minutes, bath the mix with soy and oyster sauce, these being in a 3:4 ratio, if possible
  9. Add the fried egg
  10. Fry for 1-2 more minutes
  11. Sprinkle in some pepper while frying
